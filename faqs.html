Where do we product our products?

Do we use any animal-based ingredients to make our plant-based drinks?

Our company is located in the heart of the Biosphere Reserve in Montseny National Park. This allows us to make our drinks with a type of water ideal for this purpose, with a low sodium content. Water is the main component of our plant-based drinks, so this prime location is key to ensuring the characteristic flavour of our drinks.

All of our products are naturally free from lactose and casein (milk protein). They are all free from ingredients sourced from animals, making them suitable for a vegetarian or vegan diet, as they do not contain any milk derivatives or traces of milk or any other animal products.
